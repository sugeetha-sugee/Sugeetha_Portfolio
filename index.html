About Me

Hello! I’m a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student with a strong passion for full-stack web development and creating impactful digital solutions.

I have successfully completed a Full Stack Development internship, where I gained hands-on experience in developing and deploying web applications. During this time, I worked on real-time projects covering both frontend and backend development.
